About Steminist

Our mission is to encourage more women to go into STEM to ensure that women can get equal opportunities, and help narrow the pay gap etc.

Why Steminist?

- There is lack of women in the STEM Field because of lack of role models in women in stem, stereotypes, and sexism.


- This website will expose younf females into STEM and help support women by providing new learning opportunities in the community.


- Teenagers these days are the best people to truly understand the importance of women in STEM.


- Giving women equal opportunities to various STEM careers can help for various reasons. While educating teenagers on the role that women have played in making space for us in the STEM field.


- We also have included various resources on how to get into STEM, the history of women in STEM, reputable articles, videos, and a page for women activists in STEM!!
